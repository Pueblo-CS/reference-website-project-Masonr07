Allen Iverson's upbringing


Allen Ezail Iverson was born June 7 1975 in Virginia Hampton in a very poor part of the city growing up in the projects with his mom and two sisters Ann Iverson(mother) Brandy and liesha. they would struggle to make ends often going without electricity or water Iverson would often take care of his sistes while still atending highschool.
